Brown Residences
  • Total Stores: 90 Units
  • Total Budget: $8,00,000.00
  • Contractor Used: Kue Sus LLC.
  • Project Planned for: December 2023.

This new project presents a challenge to our building planners, as we are going for a classical look on the building while still maintainig the structure of modern minimalism.

Modern minimalism and Classical building are very different and opposite building theories, however we plan on focusing the structure on minimalism while the decoration and colouring as classical.
